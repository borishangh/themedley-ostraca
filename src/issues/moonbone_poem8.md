Moonbone...

Moogy moon singin ,
I got a moonbone all 4 you, Dollin ,

N tha moon’s Maybees singin back ,
a-buzz , buzz , buzz...
Pert-A — insert Pert-P...

Macun erp der wurds naw...
You ole moonbone u
Cus it’s all mood n no mouth

der moon...it’s in yer hed
It’s in yer
