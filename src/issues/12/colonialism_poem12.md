they found the body
alive - of course - breathing as if asleep
eyes closed
some said it had an athletes form
tall and powerfully limbed
some say a child
others a young woman
naked and wet for fucking
they stared at the body all wanting it
but for different reasons
under different stars they chattered
but none could decide who had the best reason
so eventually they took their saws and knives
to the body breathing as if asleep
carving and slicing
and they stood in the blood
and held their dripping portions aloft
all proudly saying
"See this?
This is mine"
