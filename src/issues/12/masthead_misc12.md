Rooted in the heart of India, she identifies as an ambivert—someone who feels deeply, observes quietly, and expresses through words. <br>With a soft spot for dogs and an ever-present love for music, she finds comfort in the little things that make life feel whole. Literature and poetry remain her forté, where words become her paint and the world her canvas. Through every line she writes, Ishita doesn’t just aim to tell stories, she seeks to feel them, live them, and let them breathe.<br>She hopes her journey is one of compassion and purpose, where her words don’t just exist, but gently shape a kinder, more thoughtful world.</div>
